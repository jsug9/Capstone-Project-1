Hello, Rockers!

Break Out used to be known as Morosso, the band decided it was time for a rebranding, and now they're back with more power than ever before. The band will offer a concert to show their brand new album.

2022.03.12(SAT) ~ 13(SUN)

@ Beer Garden

Main Program

Opening

Attendants will have time to accommodate and buy some food while the band does a last rehearse.

Talking

The band will talk about the rebranding as well as their inspiration behind their new album.

Live Concert

The so awaited new album will be performed live for the first time.

Album Signing

The band will make personalized signs on the albums of the attendants.

Forum

Attendants will have the chance to ask their questions to the band members.
